The problem isn't supply. It's the missing layer in between.

Every asset speaks its own language. Every platform is its own island. There is no shared layer for a producer and a consumer to discover each other, agree, exchange, verify, and settle.

We have seen this before.

Once, isolated networks could not speak to each other — until a shared layer connected them all. We called it the internet. Energy is still waiting for its version.
